# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** 2026-01-27

The "Late Labels" extension respects your privacy.

## Data Collection
1. **Local Processing:** This extension reads attendee names displayed on the Google Calendar page solely to assign a random or user-defined "arrival estimate" label.
2. **No Data Transmission:** We do not transmit, sell, or sync any user data, calendar details, or attendee names to any external servers.
3. **Local Storage:** Any customizations (such as manually setting a label for a specific attendee) are stored using the Chrome Extension Storage API (`chrome.storage.local` or `chrome.storage.sync`) directly on your device.

## Permissions
- **Host Permission (`https://calendar.google.com/*`):** Required to inject the humorous labels into the calendar interface.
- **Storage:** Required to remember your settings and label customizations.
